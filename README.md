# Opus Academica

*A modular-building eurogame for 2–4 players · ~2 hours*

Opus Academica is a worker-placement and engine-building board game set in the village of
**Sileatus**, where a newly founded academy of magic is being raised over a natural spring of
Aether. Each player leads a guild of builders — a **Magister** and three **Apprentices** —
competing to become the academy's most celebrated architect. You send your workers to operate
the campus buildings, construct new ones onto a shared, growing board, and chase the
**Prestige Points (PP)** that lead to victory.

This repository holds the **rules and card catalog**, in Spanish and English. It's a game in
development; the texts here are the player-facing reference.

## How it plays (in brief)

- **Work** — place an Apprentice or your Magister on a building's time track. When the worker
  reaches the end of the track it produces, and you collect the building's effect (resources,
  cards, and more).
- **Build** — an Apprentice constructs a card from your hand onto an empty space orthogonally
  adjacent to the existing board. Buildings are **Academies** or **Courtyards**, and how they
  sit next to each other matters.
- **Supervise** — your Magister speeds one of your Apprentices forward along its track.
- **Objectives** — personal goals that give you a strategic direction and award extra PP when
  completed.
- The game ends at the end of the round in which a player reaches **35 PP** (buildings +
  objectives). Most Prestige Points wins.

Resources: **Mundane (M)**, **Gold (G)**, **Aether (A)**, **Insight (I)**.

## Print & Play

Ready-to-print PDFs are attached to the
[latest release](https://github.com/mateobur/opusacademica/releases/latest):

- **[Card deck (EN)](https://github.com/mateobur/opusacademica/releases/latest/download/opusacademica_cards_en.pdf)**
  — 84 building cards + 30 objective cards, poker size (63.5 × 88.9 mm), 9 per A4 sheet.
  *(Large file — full-resolution art.)*
- **[Brotherhood board (EN)](https://github.com/mateobur/opusacademica/releases/latest/download/opusacademica_brotherhood_board_en.pdf)**
  — the personal player board, 2 per A4 sheet.

Print on **A4, portrait, 100% scale** (default or minimum margins).
