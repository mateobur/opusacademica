OPUS ACADEMIA — CARD CATALOG

Conventions:

  • Cost = build cost
  • Entry = cost to work on the card
  • Immediate track = produces in Phase 2 of this round
  • One-round track = produces next round
  • Two-round track = produces two rounds from now

The 6 neutral cards of the starting board give no PP.

Objectives

Objectives give additional PP on completion. Each player may have up to 2 active objectives simultaneously. They are obtained in the initial draft and from the Chancellery. The goal for some objectives varies with the number of players (shown as 2p/3p/4p).

COMMUNITY SERVICE — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: Other players work on your private buildings 6/8/10 times.

NETWORK OF FAVORS — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: Work on private buildings of other players 6/8/8 times. In 3-4 players, it must be on buildings of at least 2 different rivals.

ATTENTIVE MENTOR — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Your Magister supervises 5 times.

LANDHOLDER — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Have 3 owned Courtyards orthogonally connected.

EMBASSY — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Build a card with at least 2 adjacent rival cards. Completes on build.

IMPROVED VERSION — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Build a building that a rival has already built. Completes on build.

INAUGURAL BANQUET — 3 PP | Mixed

  • Goal: Build an Academy of 5+ PP adjacent to Dining Hall or The Tavern.

WELCOME CEREMONY — 3 PP | Mixed

  • Goal: Your Magister produces at The Great Gate.

LANDMARK BUILDING — 3 PP | Solo (x5)

  • Goal: One of your Academies ends with all 4 orthogonal sides occupied and at least 1 adjacent Courtyard. Place a Landmark Building token on that Academy.

INSPIRED IDEA — 3 PP | Interactive (x2)

  • Goal: Place 1 card from your hand on this objective. If another player builds it (free action), you complete the objective.

WELL OF WISDOM — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Your Insight + cards in hand sum to 10 or more.

LOAN — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: On acquisition, deposit 2 Gold. You may only pick this objective if you can make that deposit. Another player may take them (free action) to complete the objective.

INSTITUTIONAL COLLABORATION — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: Other players deposit Insight on this card 2/3/4 times. Each time they do, the collaborator draws 2 cards.

REPRIMAND — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: Advance 1 space a rival Apprentice that is not on a production space. Free action, once per turn. 3 times to complete.

SPLENDID — 3 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Spend 5 or more Gold in a single round.

GREATER CLOISTER — 4 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Have 3 owned Academies orthogonally connected.

VILLAGE WORKERS — 3 PP | Interactive (x2)

  • Goal: Accumulate 3 Gold on this card. Any rival may deposit 1 Gold (free action, 1 time per turn) and receive 2 Mundane in exchange.

PRIVATE DEPARTMENT — 4 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Have 4 owned buildings in a straight line, horizontal or vertical.

HAUGHTY — 4 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Build an Academy adjacent to a rival Academy, with at least 2 PP more than it. Completes on build.

FIXER — 3 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: On acquisition, place 4 resources from the bank on this objective in any combination of Mundane/Gold/Aether/Insight. During their turn, each rival may take 1 resource from this objective as a free action, at most 1 per turn. You may not take resources from your own objective. When no resources remain on this objective, complete it.

GUEST OF HONOR — 4 PP | Interactive

  • Goal: Work 4 times on a building owned by a rival with 5 or more PP.

GARDEN PASSAGE — 4 PP | Solo

  • Goal: Have an Academy-Courtyard-Academy line, all owned by you.

BROTHERHOOD HOUSE — 4 PP | Solo (x2)

  • Goal: Your 4 meeples (3 Apprentices + Magister) are working or supervising on buildings you own at the same time.
